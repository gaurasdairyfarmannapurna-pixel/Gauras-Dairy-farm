❓ Q&As / Help Hub

🥛 What is Milk Fat & how is it calculated?

Fat represents the natural rich cream content in the milk. In Nainwa, we calculate it using automated centrifugal milk testing machines. A milk sample is spun to separate the lipids, giving an exact percentage. Our raw Sahiwal Cow milk ranges between 3.5% - 4.5% fat, while Murrah Buffalo milk is a thick 6% - 8% fat.

🧪 What is SNF and Lactometer Degree?

SNF (Solids-Not-Fat) represents the dry nutrients in milk excluding fat (such as proteins, calcium, lactose, and minerals). For premium raw milk, the target SNF should be between 9% to 11%. Degree is the density reading measured using a lactometer. Pure whole milk has a degree of 26 to 30. This density test is our guarantee that no water has been artificially blended into your milk.

🐄 Sahiwal Cow vs. Murrah Buffalo milk?

Sahiwal Cross Cow Milk is famous for being A2-type, light, sweet, and highly digestible. It is perfect for children, elderly, and daily drinking. Premium Murrah Buffalo Milk is highly rich and viscous, loaded with organic fats. It is the pinnacle choice for making thick curd, paneer, and rich yellow ghee.

💰 Why is our Milk priced at ₹70?

Unlike massive commercial milk processors, we do not standardise or skim fat. We do not extract cream to sell separately, nor do we dilute the milk to multiply volume. The ₹70 price represents 100% pure, raw, whole cow and buffalo milk straight from the animal. This price directly sustains high-quality dry fodder, local farm workers, and professional veterinarian care for our herd.

🏠 How should I store Gauras Raw Milk?

Because our milk contains zero preservatives or chemical additives, you should boil it immediately upon delivery. After boiling, refrigerate it below 4°C. Raw milk stored this way remains perfectly sweet and fresh for up to 48 hours.

Gauras Herd Manager App

Maintain daily milk yields, vaccine timetables, dry fodder levels, and automated monthly invoices in one clean digital companion logbook built specifically for local dairy farms.

✨ Key Features

🥛 Daily Milk yield logging

Record morning and evening yields per cattle. Automatically charts individual lactation curves and highlights cows with drop in yields.

🌾 Dry fodder & Feed monitor

Track dry fodder stocks, concentrate feed ratios, mineral supplies, and silage weights. Predicts fodder run-out dates dynamically.

🩺 Vet table & Health card

Reminds you of artificial insemination dates, upcoming vaccinations, and deworming schedules. Logs veterinary treatments per animal.

📊 billing & subscriber lists

Manage delivery routes and logs. With one click, compiles milk cards and generates monthly WhatsApp invoice invoices for your customers.
